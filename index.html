EXTRAORDINARY EXPERIENCES

 

Set on one of the longest coast lines in Kenya, the award winning beach resort has a modern Swahili twist to all the facilities.

Swimming Pools

Our 5 swimming pools include a 'silent' pool dedicated to those who just need to wind down by the pool with a book, a pool with a bar and one with a slide.

 

Tulia Wellness Spa

Our Spa offers Ayurvedic, Western and Oriental treatments. The Ayurvedic treatments are based on an ancient Indian form of holistic healing where health is a result of harmony in body, mind and spirit.

 

Tulia Fitness Center

Exercise equipment includes cross trainers, exercise cycles, free weights, multi gym, rowing machines and treadmills.

 

Ozone Kids Club

Children love the Sarova Whitesands Beach Resort & Spa, a popular getaway destination on the Northern coast for families. A wide variety of in and out-door activities are planned and the property has its very own children's playground.

 

Baby Sitting

A babysitter service is available should parents want to enjoy a relaxing romantic evening together. Our staff and management team are determined to offer a relaxed, safe and cheerful resort where children and parents alike can feel at ease.

 

Our Luxurious Rooms & Suites

With quaint Swahili architecture, elegant furnishings and modern amenities, our rooms provide contemporary comfort that makes for a relaxing & memorable stay by the beach. Designed with family comfort in mind, our spacious rooms each feature a treasured balcony for amazing views of the ocean, pools and our tropical gardens.

Tulia Spa

"Tulia" is a Swahili word which means to relax and at our Tulia Spa, you’re assured of just that. Set in a lush tropical garden, our spa boasts 2 treatment rooms with ocean views, a quiet pool and a rejuvenating jacuzzi. Tulia Spa is designed to ensure your mind, body and spirit are completely rejuvenated.

3. When the sales conditions for the reserved rate or package allow:
- The reservation can be cancelled via the “Contact Us” section on www.luxresorts.com website or by contacting Customer Services on the contact details stated above in Article 9 in Terms and Conditions.
- Changes to the reservation can be made with Customer Services, whose contact details are stated on the reservation confirmation sent by email and in Article 9 above.
4. In the event that the stay is interrupted, the agreed price will be paid in full. No reimbursement will be given for a prepaid reservation.
5. Unless expressly stated otherwise, the customer must vacate the room before 12 p.m. on the last day of the reservation, unless otherwise stated in the rate conditions booked. Failure to do so shall result in the customer being billed for an extra night.
